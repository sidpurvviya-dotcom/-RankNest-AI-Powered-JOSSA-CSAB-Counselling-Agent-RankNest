# #RankNest: AI-Powered JoSAA/CSAB Admission Agent RankNest is an advanced admission strategy agent engineered to modernize the complex JoSAA and CSAB counselling process. Designed for engineering aspirants who face high-stakes academic decisions, RankNest acts as a personal consultant, transforming raw historical cutoff data into precise, actionable intelligence.

The Problem Engineering aspirants often struggle to balance their performance in entrance exams with the realities of seat allocation. Many students waste high ranks by filling choices inefficiently or failing to account for the nuance between Home State (HS) and Other State (OS) quotas.

The Solution: An Intelligent Agent Unlike basic prediction tools, RankNest employs a predictive analysis engine that goes beyond simple lookup functions. By evaluating a user's rank against multi-year opening and closing rank datasets, the agent autonomously performs:

Tiered Risk Assessment: Classifying colleges into "Safe" (guaranteed), "Target" (highly probable), and "Reach" (aspirational) categories.

Trend-Aware Filtering: Implementing a dynamic 10% safety buffer to account for record-high competition levels in recent years.

Quota-Specific Optimization: Ensuring that category-specific reservations and state-based quotas are prioritized, preventing the misalignment often seen in manual choice filling.

Strategic Sequencing: Generating an optimized "Choice Filling" order that maximizes the student's probability of securing their most preferred branch and institution.

Technical Architecture RankNest is built on a robust MERN stack (MongoDB, Express, React, Node.js), ensuring a seamless and responsive user experience. The "intelligence" is powered by a custom-prompted LLM agent that processes the student’s profile—including rank, category, and state—to deliver high-accuracy guidance. This architecture allows the system to not just report data, but to act as a strategic partner throughout the student's journey.

Impact By automating the complex logic of seat allocation, RankNest empowers students to make data-backed decisions with confidence. Whether a student is aiming for a top-tier NIT or exploring options through the CSAB special rounds, RankNest provides the clarity needed to navigate the competition effectively.
